# CMEfy Landing Page

A bespoke one-pager prepared for CMEfy ahead of eMerge Americas 2026 (Silver booth, exhibitor 062). The page carries the orchestration hypothesis from the fit analysis in CMEfy's own visual voice: Roboto, the CMEfy blue palette, Material-Design-style card surfaces on pale blue ground, and the clinician-first tone of their marketing and product sites.

No JavaScript. No external fonts beyond Roboto (already used across cmefy.com and about.cmefy.com). All tokens sourced from `brand-pack.json`.

Intended URL path: /for/cmefy
